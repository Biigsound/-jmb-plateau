WHO IS HE?

A Servant-Leader
Rooted in Faith

Chief Dr. Jonathan Musa Bature is a distinguished statesman, accomplished professional, and passionate advocate for the people of Plateau State. With credentials including CPSM, CINTAS, FNIS, and FCILT, he brings decades of excellence in leadership and public service.

A man of deep faith who truly believes that "ONE WITH GOD..." is majority. He is committed to bringing transformative governance, security, economic empowerment, and infrastructural development to the Plateau Senatorial District.
